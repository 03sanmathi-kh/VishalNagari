Course Reflections

Working on this city project was like trying to organize a city with a spaghetti of algorithms—but somehow, it clicked! The DAA course taught me how to break big, messy problems into manageable pieces, optimize solutions, and think critically about efficiency—basically, turning chaos into a city that runs (almost) smoothly.

From adaptive resource flow to disaster-resilient infrastructure, every module was a mix of coding, creativity, and a dash of real-world problem-solving. Sure, some simulations threw tantrums, and some ideas needed multiple tries, but every hiccup was a lesson, and every tiny success felt joyfyl.

This project didn’t just sharpen my technical skills,it showed me that behind every number, there’s a story, and behind every algorithm, an opportunity to make a real impact. It has helped me to grow better as a thinker, a learner, and a problem-solver… and I might even laugh at a bug or two along the way.
