Module 2: Crop Recommendation & Seasonal Planning System

Problem Statement

Many farmers choose crops without analyzing soil type, water availability, or climatic needs, leading to lower yield. The Crop Recommendation and Seasonal Planning System aims to assist farmers in selecting suitable crops based on soil conditions and seasonal factors in Vishalnagari. Traditional crop selection often relies on experience rather than data-driven analysis, leading to reduced yield. This module organizes crop information systematically and evaluates suitability for different seasons. The objective is to improve crop productivity, optimize land usage, and increase farmer profitability.

Solution

Binary Search to quickly match soil type to suitable crops.

Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about soil type, crop suitability, seasonal conditions, and expected yield for Vishalnagari fields. Each entry maps soil type, season, and crop with a recommendation score.

Algorithms Used

Algorithm: Binary Search on crop-soil database (O(log n))
