Museum Footfall Prediction

In this module, the focus is on predicting visitor footfall in a museum using historical data and patterns. The goal is to help museum management optimize staffing, ticketing, and crowd management.

Algorithm Used:

Pattern Recognition, Memoization, and Recursion are used to identify trends and predict peak hours. (Optional: Dynamic Programming can also be applied for further optimization.)

Algorithm Efficiency: O(n) for pattern recognition, memoization reduces repeated computations
