Our Vision

Designing Tomorrow, One Algorithm at a Time

Vishalanagari, located in Maharashtra’s Vidarbha region between Amravati and Akola, is a next-generation smart city built on the fertile lands of the Deccan Plateau. The Purna River supports rich agriculture, making the region India’s premier cotton-growing and textile hub with major export networks. This is the most unique and the only city with 100% electric vehicles within the city-range ( ensuring zero-emission transport), while manufacturing the needed electric vehicles itself. The smart algorithms manage traffic, agriculture, industries, commercial hubs, governance systems, and sustainability operations — forming a balanced and efficient urban ecosystem.
Strong roots, smart shoots.
