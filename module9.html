Module 8: Worker Knowledge Transfer System

Problem Statement

In Vishalanagari's bustling factories and service hubs, invaluable tacit knowledge — quick fixes, machine-specific tips, and local heuristics — evaporates during shift handovers. Incoming workers waste time rediscovering solutions, errors reappear, and overall throughput suffers. We need a gentle, intelligent keeper of workplace wisdom that captures on-the-floor insights in real-time, organizes them for instant lookup, and hands the right guidance to the next person on duty. The system should prioritize critical notes, surface recent shift highlights, and make finding relevant knowledge as natural as typing a single keyword. The result: fewer repeated mistakes, faster problem resolution, and a workforce that learns as it works.

Algorithms Used

Trie: Stores worker notes and keywords in a prefix-indexed structure, allowing instant retrieval even from partial or fuzzy inputs. It ensures that workers can quickly locate advice using only fragments of a word or phrase.

Skip List: Maintains chronological shift updates with fast insertion and lookup. Its layered structure makes it ideal for surfacing the most recent insights during shift handovers.

Red-Black Tree: Organizes validated insights by priority or severity, ensuring balanced and predictable access time. This helps workers instantly view the most important or urgent knowledge items.

2-3 Tree: Groups related categories of knowledge into a perfectly balanced tree. This ensures uniform search performance and helps cluster similar insights together for easier navigation.

Algorithm Efficiency

Trie: Insert & search → O(m), where m = length of input.

Skip List: Insert / delete / search → Expected O(log n).

Red-Black Tree: Insert / delete / search → Guaranteed O(log n).

2-3 Tree: Search / insert / delete → Strict O(log n) due to perfect balancing.

How This Aligns with SDGs

SDG 8 — Decent Work & Economic Growth: Preserves practical skills and reduces downtime, helping workers perform better and industries run smoother.

SDG 9 — Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure: Builds robust digital infrastructure for knowledge continuity and resilient operations.
