Module 8: Smart Energy-Efficient Street Lighting System

Problem Statement

In emerging smart cities, streetlights often waste electricity by staying ON during low traffic periods or in sparsely populated zones. The challenge is to create a real-time, adaptive system that:
  • Monitors pedestrian, vehicle, and industrial activity across the city.
  • Activates only the necessary streetlights while maintaining safety.
  • Minimizes energy consumption without compromising coverage.
  • Adapts dynamically to sudden changes in city activity, such as festivals or traffic surges.

Algorithms Used

1. Queue-Based Event Simulation: Processes city activity events (traffic, pedestrians, industry) in chronological order. This enables the system to decide which streetlights to switch ON/OFF in real-time, focusing only on zones affected by current events.

2. Union-Find (Disjoint Set Union): Groups streetlights into connected clusters based on activity zones. Ensures that only essential lights per cluster are turned ON, reducing redundancy and optimizing energy usage.

Algorithm Efficiency

Queue-Based Event Simulation: Each event is processed in O(1) time using a standard queue; highly efficient for dynamic, real-time updates.

Union-Find: Nearly O(1) per union/find operation with path compression. Clusters high-activity zones efficiently, ensuring minimal streetlights are active while maintaining coverage.

Design Techniques Used

Event-Driven Simulation: Only process changes when events occur, avoiding unnecessary calculations and making the system responsive in real-time.

Cluster-Based Optimization: Groups streetlights in connected zones, reducing energy use while keeping safety intact.

How This Aligns with SDGs

SDG 7 – Affordable & Clean Energy: Reduces energy waste by smartly controlling streetlight usage.

SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities & Communities: Ensures safe, well-lit streets while promoting energy efficiency in urban planning.

SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure: Uses advanced algorithmic solutions to optimize urban infrastructure intelligently.
