Module 1: Smart Irrigation & Water Distribution Network

Problem Statement

Farmers often waste water because pipelines do not deliver water through the shortest or least-pressure-loss routes. This leads to unequal distribution and water scarcity.

Solution

Use Dijkstra’s Algorithm to find the shortest and most efficient pipeline route so that water reaches all connected fields with minimal loss.

Algorithm Used

Dijkstra’s Algorithm is used to compute the shortest path in the water pipeline network, minimizing pressure loss and distance.

Time Complexity: O(E + V log V)

SDGs Used

  • SDG 6 – Clean Water and Sanitation
  • SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production

Impact Analysis

Optimized irrigation ensures fair water distribution, reduces wastage, improves crop yield, and supports sustainable agricultural practices.
