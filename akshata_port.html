Project Reflection!!

This project helped me understand real-world traffic management and how algorithms and data structures can optimize road usage. I used graphs, arrays, queues, heaps, BFS, Dijkstra, and Merge Sort to analyze traffic data and prioritize congested roads. I learned to break complex problems into smaller modules, implement efficient algorithms, and make real-time traffic decisions. The system supports EV routing, congestion reduction, and smart signal control, making cities more sustainable and efficient. Overall, the project enhanced my problem-solving, coding, and analytical skills while connecting theory to practical urban transport solutions.
